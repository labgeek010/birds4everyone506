Birds for EVERYONE

All people, regardless of their conditions or limitations, have the right to enjoy the beautiful birds that fill our lives with color and joy.

Get Started

Dive into Costa Rica's night birds with us.

"Experience Costa Rica's birds in a new light, literally! Our day and night sections cater to the rhythm of the jungle. Day shifts reveal the vibrant colors of diurnal birds, while night shifts uncover the mysteries of nocturnal species under the moonlit canopy."

Day and Night Birdwatching Experiences:

"Join our guided day tours to witness the lively activities of Costa Rican birds in the sunlight. As the sun sets, transition into the enchanting world of night birdwatching, where owls, nightjars, and other nocturnal creatures come to life. It's a 24-hour immersion into the captivating avian realm."

"Whether you're an early riser eager to catch the morning chorus or a night owl seeking the thrill of nocturnal discoveries, our day and night sections ensure an unforgettable birdwatching experience tailored to your preferred time and pace."
